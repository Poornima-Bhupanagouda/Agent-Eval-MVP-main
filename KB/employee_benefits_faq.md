# Employee Benefits FAQ

## Health Insurance

**Q: When does health insurance coverage begin?**
A: Coverage begins on the first day of the month following your hire date. If hired on the 1st of a month, coverage begins that same day.

**Q: What health plans are available?**
A: We offer three plans:
- **PPO Plan**: $150/month employee-only, $400/month family. $500 deductible, 80/20 coinsurance after deductible.
- **HMO Plan**: $100/month employee-only, $300/month family. $250 deductible, $20 copay for office visits.
- **High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP)**: $75/month employee-only, $200/month family. $1,500 deductible, eligible for HSA contributions.

**Q: Is dental and vision included?**
A: Dental and vision are separate plans. Dental costs $25/month and covers two cleanings per year plus 80% of major procedures. Vision costs $10/month and covers an annual eye exam plus $200 toward frames or contacts.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

**Q: Does the company match 401(k) contributions?**
A: Yes. The company matches 100% of the first 3% and 50% of the next 2% of your salary that you contribute, for a maximum match of 4% of salary.

**Q: When am I eligible for 401(k)?**
A: You are eligible after 90 days of employment. The company match vests over 3 years: 33% after year 1, 66% after year 2, 100% after year 3.

**Q: What is the maximum contribution?**
A: The IRS limit for 2024 is $23,000, or $30,500 if you are age 50 or older (catch-up contribution).

## Life Insurance

**Q: Is life insurance provided?**
A: Yes. The company provides basic life insurance at 1x your annual salary at no cost. You may purchase supplemental coverage up to 5x salary at group rates.

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

**Q: What is the EAP?**
A: The EAP provides free, confidential counseling and support services including:
- Up to 8 counseling sessions per year (in-person or virtual)
- Legal consultation (30-minute sessions)
- Financial planning assistance
- Childcare and eldercare referrals

**Q: How do I access the EAP?**
A: Call the EAP hotline at 1-800-555-0199, available 24/7. No referral or pre-authorization needed.

## Tuition Reimbursement

**Q: Does the company offer tuition reimbursement?**
A: Yes. Full-time employees with at least 1 year of service may receive up to $5,250 per year for job-related courses at accredited institutions. A grade of B or higher is required for reimbursement.

## Wellness Program

**Q: What wellness benefits are available?**
A: The company offers:
- $500 annual wellness stipend (gym membership, fitness classes, wellness apps)
- Free annual biometric screening
- Smoking cessation program (fully covered)
- Mental health days: 2 additional days per year specifically for mental wellness

## Commuter Benefits

**Q: Are commuter benefits available?**
A: Yes. You may set aside up to $300/month pre-tax for public transit or $300/month pre-tax for parking through the commuter benefit program. Enroll through the benefits portal.

## Open Enrollment

**Q: When is open enrollment?**
A: Open enrollment runs from November 1-15 each year. Changes take effect January 1. Outside of open enrollment, you may only change plans during a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, loss of other coverage).
