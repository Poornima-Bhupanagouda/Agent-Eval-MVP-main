# Company Policy Document

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Remote work arrangements must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance.

## Time Off Policy
- Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) annually
- PTO accrues monthly at a rate of 1.67 days
- Unused PTO can be carried over up to 5 days into the next year
- Time off requests require 2 weeks advance notice

## Expense Reimbursement
- Business travel expenses are reimbursed within 30 days of submission
- Meals during travel: up to $75 per day
- Mileage reimbursement: $0.67 per mile
- All expenses over $50 require receipts

## IT Security
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days
- Two-factor authentication is required for all corporate systems
- Personal devices must be registered with IT before accessing company resources
- VPN required for all remote access
